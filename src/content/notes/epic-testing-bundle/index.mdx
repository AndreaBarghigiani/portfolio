## Overview

The Epic Testing Bundle explores modern testing practices across unit, integration, and end-to-end strategies. These notes capture the major ideas, personal insights, and links to supporting material from each module.

## Goals for this course

- Understand the guiding principles behind reliable automated tests.
- Practice configuring modern tooling for frontend and full-stack applications.
- Document actionable workflows to bring back into day-to-day projects.
